THE VOTES THAT WILL TURN THE MEDIA SPACE UPSIDE DOWN!

Unique feature of the kaif platform is blockchain-based voting

This means that simultaneously all the nodes of the platform, operating on the same cryptographic algorithm, must confirm changes in the status of any vote, rating or poll for these changes to be recognized as valid.

  • Anyone can always verify any transaction in the blockchain, and the state of the blockchain is immutable and cannot be deleted
  • The summing up takes place automatically on the platform, which eliminates the risk of errors and falsifications
